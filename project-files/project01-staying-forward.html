Project 01 Staying Forward
Parsons
Digital Fabrication
2024
As an over thinker, I spend a lot of my days reminiscing and regretting the past, preventing myself from being able to enjoy the present and look forward into the future. One of my favorite songs, "People" by AgustD, always reassures me whenever I fall into this endless cycle. Through this song, I was able to try to not linger too much on the things that have already happened, since regretting only keeps me contained in the past and unable to move forward, learn from my mistakes, and grow as an individual.

Inspired by his song, I decided to create a piece that expressed my desire of 'staying forward.' I was reminded of a monocle, an object that people use to make their vision clearer, and a masquerade mask that is able to hide one's face and identity. In addition, I thought of a watch because when I regret things, I always wish to be able to turn back time and redo that scene again, in order to change result/outcome. Therefore, I reconstructed the mechanical components inside a watch into a vision lense that would help me to break away from being stuck in time and look forward to the future to keep the motion of time endlessly moving.
